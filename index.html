How it works
01
Three steps, no faff

A call comes in while you're under a sink. Here's exactly what happens.

Step 01

Your customer rings

They call your number. You're on a job, under a sink, up a roof, in the van. Mae picks up in under two rings — before it goes to voicemail.

Step 02

Mae takes the details

She asks what the problem is, how urgent it is, where they are, and whether it's gas, electrical, or plumbing. She knows the language: "boiler losing pressure," "RCD keeps tripping," "first-fix on a new bathroom." She doesn't quote prices — she always says "I'll get him to call you back with a price." No scripts. No menu. No robots.

Step 03

Job lands in your WhatsApp

Name, number, postcode, job type, urgency, time they called. All in one WhatsApp message. One tap to call them back. Done before you're off the ladder.

FAQ
04

Your questions, straight answers.

Will my customers know it's AI?

No — and we don't pretend otherwise either. Mae introduces herself by name, sounds natural, and gets the job done. Most callers won't think twice. If asked directly, she's honest.

Will Mae quote my customers a price?

Never. Mae's job is to take the details and tell the customer you'll call back with pricing. She'll never make up a number, never guess, never commit you to a job value. It's a hard guardrail — we built it on purpose.

Does she handle different UK accents?

Mae is tested on Geordie, Scouse, Brummie, Glaswegian, Welsh, multicultural London, and most regional varieties. If she ever struggles with a regular customer's accent, we retrain her on real recordings from your line in the weekly review.

What if she gets something wrong?

Every call is reviewed by a real person in your first two weeks, and a sample is reviewed every week after that. We catch slips and retrain the agent on your patterns. If a job comes through wrong, we make it right.

How long does setup take?

48 hours from the moment we have your number and a 20-minute intro call. Done-for-you. You don't touch a dashboard.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. 30 days' notice. No long contract. No exit fee.

What trades do you work with?

Plumbers, electricians, and gas engineers — that's it for now. Mae is trained on the language of those trades specifically: first-fix vs second-fix, RCD trips, Gas Safe checks, powerflushes, boiler breakdowns vs services.

What happens to the call recordings?

Stored encrypted in the UK for 90 days, then deleted. Used only for QA and agent improvement. Never sold. Never shared. ICO registered, GDPR compliant.
