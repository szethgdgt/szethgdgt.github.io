What Retinal will and won't do

What Retinal does

The job of a good office manager.

Takes calls professionally. Gets the right information. Sends you a lead. Stays out of your way. Knows your trade inside out.

  • Answers unanswered and engaged calls
  • Speaks naturally — no menus, no robots
  • Takes name, number, postcode, job type, urgency
  • Handles trade vocabulary correctly
  • Sends a structured WhatsApp message every time
  • Records every call for your review
  • Works across plumbing, electrical, and gas
What Retinal won't do

The things only you should decide.

Retinal is a receptionist, not a salesperson or estimator. It knows its lane. You stay in charge of the decisions that matter.

  • Quote a price for any job
  • Confirm availability or book a date
  • Make commitments on your behalf
  • Discuss call-out charges
  • Give technical advice or diagnose jobs
  • Pretend it's a human if asked directly
