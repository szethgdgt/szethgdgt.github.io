FAQ
04

Common questions

Will my customers know it's AI?

No — and we don't pretend otherwise either. The agent introduces itself by name, sounds natural, and gets the job done. Most callers won't think twice. If asked directly, it's honest.

What if it gets something wrong?

Every call is reviewed by us in the first two weeks and weekly thereafter. We'll catch slips and retrain the agent on your specific patterns. If a job comes through wrong, we make it right.

How long does setup take?

48 hours from the moment we have your number and a 20-minute intro call. Done-for-you. You don't touch a dashboard.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. 30 days' notice, no long contract, no exit fee. If you stop missing calls, you stop paying.

What trades do you work with?

Plumbers, electricians, and gas engineers — that's it for now. The agent is trained on the language and qualification patterns of those trades specifically.

What happens to the call recordings?

Stored encrypted in the UK for 90 days, then deleted. Used only for QA and agent improvement. Never sold, never shared. GDPR & UK DPA 2018 compliant.
